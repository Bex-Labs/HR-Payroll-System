Structured HR delivery from self-service to audit

The system supports controlled access, employee self-service, manager approvals, HR administration, payroll operations, and traceable decision history across the employee lifecycle.

Employee Self-Service

Employees can manage leave requests, view balances, track manager decisions, review payroll history, and access payslip information from one workspace.

Manager Approval Control

Managers review assigned employees only, with decision readiness checks for leave balance, employee eligibility, duplicate requests, and team coverage.

Payroll Operations

Payroll users can work with payroll records, payslip visibility, payment preparation, payroll history, and controlled payroll outputs.

Audit & Policy Support

HR decisions, returned requests, resubmissions, access separation, and lifecycle events are structured for review and accountability.

Role-based workspaces for HR and payroll operations

Each user enters the workspace assigned to their profile, with access limited to the functions needed for their HR, payroll, management, oversight, or audit responsibility.
